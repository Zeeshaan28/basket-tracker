Google Sheets sync
Every receipt gets saved to a live Google Sheet automatically.
1
Open script.google.com and click New project
2
Delete all the code in there. Open setup-sheet.gs in Notepad, copy everything, paste it in. Press Ctrl+S to save
3
Click Deploy (top right) → New deployment
4
Click the gear icon next to "Select type" and choose Web app
5
Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone. Click Deploy
6
Click Authorise access → choose your Google account → click Advanced → Go to project (unsafe) → Allow
7
Copy the Web app URL shown (ends in /exec) and paste it below
Web app URL

Due to browser security rules the app sends data silently — it can't confirm receipt. Data will appear in your sheet within a few seconds of saving.
